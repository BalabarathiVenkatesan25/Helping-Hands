How It Works

Connecting compassionate individuals with orphanage centers in three simple steps.

1

Orphanages Post Needs

Verified orphanage centers publish detailed request items stating their priority, quantity needed, and deadlines.

2

Donors Support Requests

Donors browse active lists, choose specific items or monetary amounts, and click 'Donate' to initialize the workflow.

3

Status Tracked Transparently

Donors monitor their packages from shipping up to deliveries. Orphanages verify receipt and finalize completions.
